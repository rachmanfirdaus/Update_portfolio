On-Off Pumping Unit Status Monitoring Service at Heavy Oil Field Operations Duri Pertamina Hulu Rokan, Riau

On-Off Pumping Unit Status Monitoring Service is one of the contracts in the form of services for monitoring the on-off status of wells in the WK Rokan by procuring goods and services, namely 121 RTU panel units installed at Heavy Oil - Field Operation Duri PT. Pertamina Hulu Rokan (PHR) as project Contract Owner under the supervision of FOD - OS. The project scope for services provided under the contract includes, among other things, 24-hour online data collection including several parameter data as follows:

  • Mechanical movement data from the sucker rod pump
  • Current reading data of the sucker rod pump motor drive
  • Main power status data from MPU (Main Panel Unit)
  • Overload trip indicator status data from the MPU

Visualization of the data collection that has been obtained will be displayed on a Web-based HMI Dashboard based on a certain set of rules for monitoring online or offline well status which is integrated with push notifications via WhatsApp for every change in the well status.
